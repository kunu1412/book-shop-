Leakey's Bookshop

i have visited the place on 2nd march 2018

Leakey's Bookshop was established in 1979 and has been housed for the last 30 years in the old Gaelic Church (1793). It is Scotland s largest secondhand bookshop with 100,000 selected volumes. We have been actively buying books throughout the Highlands for well over 40 years, buying that has been exciting and fruitful.
